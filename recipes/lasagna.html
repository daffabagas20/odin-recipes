Ingredients

Meat Sauces

  • Meat: 1 lb. lean ground beef and 1/2 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • Tomato Base: 24 oz. to 28 oz. marinara sauce, crushed tomatoes, or pasta sauce
  • Aromatic: 1 medium yellow onion (diced) and 2-3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • Seasoning: Olive oil (for the pan), salt, and black pepper (to taste)

Cheese Layer

  • The Base: 15 oz. to 16 oz. whole-milk ricotta cheese (or small-curd cottage cheese)
  • Binders and Herbs: 1 large egg (helps the cheese set) and 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Cheese: 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup to \1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

The Pasta

  • Pasta: 12 oz. to 16 oz. lasagna pasta

How to Make the Lasagna

  1. Prepare the Pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna pasta
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce (I love this brand) & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layering: Start by spreading a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with noodles, a layer of the cheese mixture, and more meat sauce. Repeat these layers, finishing with sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella on top.
  5. Baking: Cover the baking dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake at 375°F for 30–40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 5–10 minutes until the cheese is bubbling and golden. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
